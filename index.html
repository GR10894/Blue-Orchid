About

Blue Orchid is a cozy, family-owned business nestled in the heart of the city.
With a passion for creating unique, bespoke garments, Megha brings years of
experience and dedication to every stitch. From custom wedding dresses to
tailored suits, Megha's attention to detail and commitment to quality have
earned her a loyal clientele. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere,
Blue Orchid is the perfect destination for anyone seeking personalized,
expert tailoring services.
